Gray Zone Warfare

Released 2024 April 30

Gray Zone Warfare is another "extraction" shooter, kinda like an alternative to Tarkov. hot climate, mosquitos 24/7 buzzing in your ear.
straight up nightmare but it delivers something new. its actually open world.

40$

Buy it

Overall

6/10

Gameplay

The quests are nicely made but its 90% just a walking simulator. gunplay is not very good and sometimes feels unresponsive

6/10

Graphics

Has a good style and detail but it all just gets ruined by the terrible optimization and performance issues

6/10

Story

Story is okay, but its kinda a mess and not really catchy. your main goal is just going to be quests

4/10

Sound

The sound effects are nicely made but guns do not sound like they pack a punch. sometimes feels like im playing airsoft

7/10

Performance

Its unreal engine 5. what else can you expect. poor nanite performance and flickery lumen will not run well without upscaling (better then stock)

3/10

Community

Very active and nice community. you can find great content creators or just random people (or enemies) that will just help you

9/10

Modding

Gray Zone Warfare does not support client modding yet. but you can create your own custom servers with mods

5/10

Updates

lots of updates but mostly just bug fixes, big updates are rare or after a wipe which is happen every 6 months

6/10

Support

The developers are very nice and responsive. they are always willing to help you and answer your questions

9/10

Value

the game is a replacement for Tarkov which actually feels somewhat good to play and you wont get jump shot by a gigachad

8/10
